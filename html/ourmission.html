Our Mission

Senergy is driven to empower buildings owners and managers to achieve energy cost savings by incentivizing tenants to engage in sustainable behavior via rent discount. Leveraging the EPA's Energy Star System, Senergy will help track and analized energy usage across properties, identify anomalies and opportunities, and alert managers and operators. The Senergy System is explicity designed to facilitate Energy Star program rebates for sustainable behavior.
